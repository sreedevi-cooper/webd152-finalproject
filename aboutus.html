About Us

History

Pacific Trail Resort, established in 1998 by Emily Johnson, is located in the tranquil Pacific Northwest. Crafted as a retreat for nature enthusiasts, it blends upscale lodging with sustainable practices, rapidly earning its reputation as a top choice for travelers desiring both luxury and a harmonious natural experience.

Mission

Our mission is to provide exceptional hiking, bird watching, and kayaking adventures while focusing on guest comfort, safety, and satisfaction. We aim to strike a perfect balance between excitement and relaxation, ensuring our guests experience a refreshing and meaningful connection with nature.
